How the Daily Challenge works

  1. One attempt per day. The day locks once you simulate your season — make the draft count.
  2. Everyone gets the same board. The draft wheel is seeded by the date, so your spins match every other player's on Earth.
  3. One special rule per day — a draft constraint, a season objective, or a locked legend to build around (all listed below).
  4. Streaks & leaderboards. Consecutive clears grow your 🔥 streak, and every run posts to that day's global leaderboard.
  5. Resets at midnight UTC. A new challenge and a fresh board every day.
